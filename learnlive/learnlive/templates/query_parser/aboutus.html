About Us

Curious? Discover. Learn.

We at Qurious believe that everyone out there can teach a skill, for which a learner exists. What we do is facilitate this interaction.

Who We Are

Are you curious about knowledge? Have you ever particpated in online learning and felt frustrated with the lack of interaction with the instructor? Have you ever had a question and wanted a quick personalized response? Have you ever yearned for in depth discussions with fellow students online? We at Qurious have and are doing something to change it. We firmly believe that everyone possesses some unique skill, and more importantly, there are people out there who actively seek to obtain this information. We are creating an open and friendly peer-to-peer platform that connects your questions to real life experts in the respective fields who bid on your questions in real time. You get the personalized help you always deserved, and you get it quick. Our platform bridges the gap between students and teachers and facilitates real-time conversation in which people can share their skills. Join us now, as a learner, as an instructor, as a curious individual!
